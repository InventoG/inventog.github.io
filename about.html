About Invento

Invento is the annual tech fest of Government Engineering College Sreekrishnapuram, Palakkad.

Invento has a rich tradition and technological importance in the state. It is held every year in the springs(February / March).

InventoG is the technical organization that manages the technical sides of the Tech fest.
